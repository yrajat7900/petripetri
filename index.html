About Petrifern

India's first
export-grade TC lab.

Petrifern is a BSL-1 certified ornamental plant tissue culture laboratory based in Dahina, Rewari, Haryana — the only facility of its kind at commercial scale in India.

We propagate seven high-value ornamental species under strict biosafety protocols, producing disease-free, genetically uniform plantlets for nurseries, exporters, and institutional growers across India and beyond.

Founded by an IIT Delhi biotechnology graduate and co-directed by a PhD-qualified plant biotechnologist specialising in orchid and aroid TC propagation.

Our Protocol

From single cell
to field-ready plant.

01

Explant Selection

Mother cultures sourced from ICAR-licensed research centres or certified international breeders. Every explant is pathogen-verified before entering the BSL-1 production environment.

02

In-Vitro Multiplication

Propagation under 35 LAF workstations using species-specific sterile media. Dual 70TR chiller units maintain optimal culture temperature around the clock.

03

Quality Control

100% visual inspection at every sub-culture stage. Full batch traceability — species identity, mother source, passage number, and contamination rate — on every production lot.

04

Hardening & Dispatch

Structured acclimatisation in controlled hardening chambers. Domestic delivery to verified buyers; exports via phytosanitary-certified cold-chain packaging.

Our Facility

Infrastructure built
for the world.

Our BSL-1 certified campus in Dahina, Rewari, Haryana is designed from the ground up for export-grade ornamental tissue culture — every specification chosen to meet international biosafety standards.

1,300 sqm of certified lab space, 35 Laminar Air Flow workstations, dual 70-Tonne Refrigeration chiller units, and a climate-controlled growth room holding 3.6 lakh jars. A compactor shelving system accommodates up to 15 lakh jars in parallel storage.
